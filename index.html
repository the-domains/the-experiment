The Experiment

The Experiment is Nick Jones and Trevor O’Brien. We found out we loved working together while making digital things in the agency world, we just didn't love the agency world. So we set out to find the sort of projects we wished we were working on, and the sort of collaborators we wished we were working with. So far, so good. Now, we ask ourselves these three questions before we take on any project...

The Experiment and The Grid

In August of last year, Leigh Taylor asked if we’d be interested in working on this crazy “AI for websites” idea he was working on with Dan Tocchini and a band of talented misfits. We said “yes.” It still kicks our ass every day. We still love it.

Question 1:

Will we be learning something we want to know?

Question 2:

Will it positively impact someone other than us and the person paying us?

Question 3:

Will we be proud to one day show this to our grandkids?
